EARTH'S MOON


Earth's Moon is the brightest and largest object in our night sky. The Moon makes Earth a more livable planet by moderating our home planet's wobble on its axis, leading to a relatively stable climate. It also causes tides, creating a rhythm that has guided humans for thousands of years.

Earth's Sun

The Sun is the star at the heart of our solar system. Its gravity holds the solar system together, keeping everything from the biggest planets to the smallest bits of debris in its orbit.

Tulip

Tulips are spring-blooming perennial herbaceous bulbiferous geophytes (having bulbs as storage organs) in the Tulipa genus. Their flowers are usually large, showy, and brightly coloured, generally red, orange, pink, yellow, or white (usually in warm colours).
